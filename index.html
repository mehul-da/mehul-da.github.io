About Me

Hey! My name is Mehul and I'm a sophomore at the University of Texas at Austin pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Mathematics.

My passion for Computer Science arose in my AP CS class in high school, where I realized my love for solving challenging problems as well as the endless possibilities of this field. Ever since, I have spent my time exploring various programming languages, working with different frameworks/APIs, and strengthening my foundation in key technical concepts.

This past summer, I interned at Dive Chat, an Austin-based tech start-up, as a Software Developer, where I had the opportunity to construct the mobile and web platforms for an innovative channel-based messaging application. In addition, I worked with InStok as a Software Engineering Intern, where I worked on a Google Chrome extension that simplifies the process of buying products online during the COVID-19 period.

My interests lie in the fields of Full-Stack Engineering, Machine Learning and Web & Mobile Application Development, through which I hope to develop effective solutions to the problems that we face in our society today. Feel free to reach out to me!
